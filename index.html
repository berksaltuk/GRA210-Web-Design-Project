Cumhuriyet'in 100'leri

Cumhuriyet, or the Republic, is not just a political system; it's a symbol of resilience, progress, and unity. Join us in celebrating the 100th anniversary of the Turkish Republic with a series of conference that pays tribute to a century of remarkable achievements.

This event is a homage to the unwavering spirit of a nation that transformed itself into a modern, democratic society. "Cumhuriyet'in 100'leri" will feature thought-provoking discussions, historical retrospectives, and a glimpse into the bright future that lies ahead. We invite you to be part of this momentous occasion during 5-7 January 2024 at Bilkent Hotel & Conference Center, as we honor the past, embrace the present, and chart a course for a future full of promise and opportunity. Come, join the conversation, and be inspired by the legacy of Cumhuriyet!

Celal Şengör, a prominent geologist, brings a profound understanding of Turkey's geological marvels. His engaging discourse on the nation's unique landscapes and geological history will leave you with a newfound appreciation for the extraordinary natural heritage of the Turkish Republic.

Mete Atatüre, a brilliant physicist, is at the forefront of quantum technology. With a dynamic and innovative approach, Mete will unveil the intriguing world of quantum science, shedding light on how it can shape the future of the Turkish Republic and the world.
